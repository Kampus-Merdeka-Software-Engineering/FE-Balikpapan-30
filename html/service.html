BEST HEALTH SERVICE

Whether you require routine medical check-ups, specialized surgeries, or emergency care, Gracefield Regional Medical Center offers a comprehensive range of services. Our departments include cardiology, orthopedics, obstetrics and gynecology, pediatrics, oncology, and many more.

WE GIVE THE BEST HEALTH SERVICE

CARDIOLOGIST

Diagnosis and treatment of heart-related conditions


CARDIOLOGIST


Dedicated to the diagnosis and treatment of heart-related conditions. Our team of skilled cardiologists, nurses, and technicians utilizes advanced cardiac imaging and state-of-the-art technology to provide comprehensive care for patients with heart diseases, ranging from routine check-ups to complex cardiac surgeries.


ORTHOPEDICS

Specializes in the musculoskeletal system


ORTHOPEDICS


Specializes in the musculoskeletal system, including bones, joints, muscles, and tendons. Our orthopedic surgeons and rehabilitation experts offer a wide range of services, from joint replacements and spine surgeries to sports medicine and physical therapy, ensuring that you can regain mobility and live a pain-free life.


OBSTETRICS

Dedicated to women's health throughout every stage of life


OBSTETRICS


Dedicated to women's health throughout every stage of life. Our experienced obstetricians provide prenatal care, deliver babies, and offer postnatal support. Additionally, our gynecologists offer a range of services, including routine check-ups, family planning, and treatment for gynecological conditions.


PEDIATRICS

Committed to the health and well-being of children


PEDIATRICS


Committed to the health and well-being of children. Our pediatricians provide expert care for infants, children, and adolescents. Services include well-child check-ups, vaccinations, treatment for childhood illnesses, and guidance for parents on child development and nutrition.


ONCOLOGY

Specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of cancer


ONCOLOGY


Specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of cancer. Our dedicated team of oncologists, nurses, and support staff offers a comprehensive approach to cancer care, including chemotherapy, radiation therapy, surgical interventions, and palliative care. We also focus on providing emotional and psychological support to patients and their families during their cancer journey.
